# 🚀 About Me

I work at the intersection of **data science, machine learning, and power systems**.

My research focuses on applying **machine learning, statistical modeling, and extreme value theory** to understand and quantify the risks of **large-scale power outage events**.

Alongside research, I build **real-world engineering systems** including **IoT devices, embedded platforms, and full data science pipelines**.
